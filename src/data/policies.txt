# Company Policies (Dummy File for QA Review)

## Subscription Management
- Users can upgrade or downgrade their plans anytime from Account Settings > Subscription.
- Upgrades take effect immediately.
- Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
-  Support agents cannot manually change plans for users.

## Payment Issues
- Accepted payment methods: Credit Card, PayPal, Bank Transfer.
- Failed payments must be retried within 7 days to avoid account suspension.
- Refunds require manager approval and cannot be promised directly to customers.
-  Agents must never say "We will refund" or give a specific refund timeline.
-  Agents shoulld say like refunding depends on manager approval. you need to contact them.
-  Agents must not request sensitive financial details (full card numbers, CVV, etc.).

## Support
- Priority support is available for Premium users.
- Users can open tickets via the Support Portal or email.
-  Do not provide personal phone numbers for support.

## Security
- Two-factor authentication (2FA) is recommended for all accounts.
- Passwords must be at least 8 characters with one special character.
- Suspicious login attempts trigger automatic account locks.
-  Agents must not unlock accounts manually or share internal security procedures.

## Data Privacy
- User data will not be shared with third parties without consent.
- Data deletion requests can be made at any time.
- Backups are retained for 30 days before permanent deletion.
-  Do not provide raw database exports directly to customers.
